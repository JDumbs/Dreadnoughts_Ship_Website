Welcome to Design Hub!

My goal is to house historical ship builds for the share design feature of Ultimate Admiral Dreadnoughts

My current goal is to start with battleships from the dates the game allows and possibly the "paper ships" meaning ships that never left the drawing board or were never completed

I am a one man team right now so I sadly wont have every ship ever put into service on launch of the site. So any help is appreciated

Please send any historical builds to admiraljdumbs@gmail.com I will have to make sure they are as accurate as possible so please don't burry me in repeat and non-historical ships

Tutorial

Step 1: Click the image asociated with the ship you want in your campain

Step 2: Open your Downloads file in your computer and check to make sure the ship file downloaded properly

Step 3: Press windows key + R and type in %appdata%

Step 4: Go to AppData then LocalLow then Game Labs then Ultimate Admiral Dreadnoughts

Step 5: Open the Designs folder then drag the new ship file in
